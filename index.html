Who Am I

ABOUT ME

Entrepreneurship, execution and inclusive growth.

Approach

Mayra Khan is an award-winning serial entrepreneur who builds companies that scale with systems and people. With 15+ years in driver education, she turns clear playbooks and tech-enabled operations into measurable growth.

Recognition AND Community

Honors include WBDC Entrepreneur of the Year, Enterprising Woman of the Year, and Chicago Business Journal Women of Influence, with appearances in Fortune and ICIC’s Inner City 100. She’s a Goldman Sachs 10,000 Small Businesses alum and a Stanford GSB Latino Entrepreneurship Initiative graduate, active in mentorship and board service advancing supplier diversity.

Companies Owned

She owns and operates Teamficient, iDrivio, Hire A Latino, and co-founded AccuSights. Her focus: applying proven systems to help small and midsize businesses scale.
